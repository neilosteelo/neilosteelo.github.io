Intro

Do you want better and more succesful outcomes in your personal or professional life?

Are you facing problems, issues or limits that prevent you from getting what you want?

Are you seeking more meaningful relationships and experiences, or simply, are you seeking meaning in your life?

Inner Space Techniques (IST) are a set of practices and tools for self-knowledge, overcoming emotional and energetic blockages, and creating excellence in your life. IST is based a on a foundation of established meditation techniques and the simple and very powerful understanding that the quality of experiences in your life is a product of your internal unconscious reactions. IST brings illumination to the inner landscape, resolution to the unconscious reactions that lie within, and leads to awakening!

Neil Arora has been practising intensive meditation and Inner Space Techniques for over 10 years and is passionate about bringing awakening and excellence to groups and individuals through the powerful medium of IST and meditation.

IST

We all find ourselves facing issues and limits in life. It is how we deal with these issues that determines what can happen next and whether our life flourishes into a work of art.

Inner Space Techniques (or IST) deals with issues at their source, meaning not with the resulting phenomena or symptoms as they appear on the surface, but the very source of the problem. Once a problem or issue is seen in IST at this causal level it is not that the problem is solved as such - rather it disappears, because the cause is no longer active.

It is usually the case that any one problem you face in life is the current manifestation of a deeper ongoing issue. IST seeks to resolve the root of this deep ongoing issue. IST is not merely a bandaid solution, but a solution that brings real change throughout your life.

IST is a profound process. Seeing and resolving issues at their root gives you access to deeper and more awakened aspects of your consciousness. Human consciousness is a broad spectrum from the discursive mind (the endless blah, blah, blah narrative), to states of inner silence and a knowing of one's self that seekers have sought after for thousands of years, and sages have attained.

Neil has been guiding people through the inner space of IST for over 10 years and has worked with people from many different walks of life, to bring freedom - freedom to be more than what they previously thought was possible. To find out more or book a session, contact Neil.

To learn more about IST, see the IST website.

Meditation

Meditation is more than simply relaxation - for thousands of years it has been used to awaken!

Meaning clarity, speed, focus.

In everyone's life there are special moments when the mind becomes unusually acute and clear, thoroughly focused, rooted in the here and now. The senses are sharp and precise. You are in a bigger part of yourself, you are alive! You ARE.

The meditation techniques taught by Neil have three fundamental principles:

  • stillness
  • silencing the mind
  • internalisation of the senses and of consciousness

Through a meditation practice of this nature you become clearer, faster, sharper, more capable of intense concentration over extended periods. Thought processes become fluid, and speed up. The heart flourishes.

It leads to awakening from inside. An awakening that doesn't flicker with external circumstances.

Increased awareness, inspiration and clarity.

Be it at work or at home, daily life becomes a different experience.

The meditation techniques offered by Neil were developed by the Clairvision School of Meditation, a worldwide School of meditation that aims to train people in meditation to a high level. The meditation techniques work hand in glove with Inner Space Techniques. Neil offers both individual sessions and group workshops. To find out more about workshops in your area, or book an individual session, contact Neil.

About

Neil is an IST Practitioner with over 10 years of experience practising the techniques of IST and meditation as taught by the Clairvision School of Meditation. On the side Neil dabbles as a lawyer, but his passion is exploring consciousness and awakening! In the past five years Neil has spent over 24 months on full-time retreats at the Clairvision School's retreat centres in Northern California and New South Wales, being trained and training others in techniques of meditation and IST.

Neil's philosophy is simple. Nearly all of life's problems arise from or relate to a disconnection from the inner flame, which is every human being's birthright. Spiritual traditions since time immemorial have aimed at uniting the mundane life with this inner flame. In all aspects of his life, including when working with individuals in a course of IST sessions or teaching meditation, this is Neil's one guiding principle: to provide opportunities for individuals to have an experience of their flame. From there, anything is possible!
